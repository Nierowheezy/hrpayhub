Who We Are

HRPayHub is a flagship product of DelonApps, a global technology and outsourcing firm with offices in Boston, USA; Lagos, Nigeria; and Liverpool, UK. At DelonApps, we're committed to transforming how businesses manage HR, payroll, and financial processes through innovative software solutions that simplify complex workflows.

Our mission is to empower SMEs, large enterprises, and independent pharmacies with tools that streamline operations, reduce administrative burdens, and ensure regulatory compliance. With years of experience in outsourcing and software development, we understand the unique challenges faced by businesses in diverse sectors, from oil and gas to healthcare and financial services.

HRPayHub is more than just a software platform—it's a strategic partner in business efficiency, bridging the gap between technology and business operations.

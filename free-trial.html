  • Different staff roles may have different pay scales.
  • NHS pension schemes require precise calculations and timely submissions.
  • Overtime, holiday pay, and locum staff wages vary month to month.
  • HMRC deadlines are strict, and penalties for non-compliance can be costly.

HRPayHub comes preloaded with UK pharmacy-specific payroll templates so you can start right away—without having to figure out complex configuration rules.

2. Nigeria Businesses

Running payroll in Nigeria can be challenging because:
  • PAYE rates vary by income brackets.
  • Pension contribution rates are mandatory and must be accurately calculated.
  • VAT rules apply differently for certain services.
  • Manual payroll risks errors that can lead to FIRS
    penalties.

HRPayHub automates these rules so that Nigerian businesses from small companies to large enterprises can process payroll accurately and on time without the stress.

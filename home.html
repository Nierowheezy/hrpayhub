Salary & Tax Remittance

Let Us Handle Your Payroll & Tax Filing — End-to-End

Free up your time and stay fully compliant. With HRPayHub's Platinum pay-as-you-go services, you can now outsource critical payroll remittances and tax filings — no need to deal with portals, queues, or deadlines.
Just pay the required amounts and we'll take it from there.

Salary Remittance

(Staff Payment)

One-click bulk salary transfers to staff bank accounts via Alat API — no banking stress, just streamlined payouts. HRPayHub handles secure disbursement using details from your payroll.

Monthly PAYE Filing

(State Revenue Service)

Pay your monthly PAYE tax amount plus a N25,000 service fee — we'll remit your taxes and file the returns with the state revenue authority. Fast, reliable, and compliant.

Annual PAYE Filing

(State Revenue Service)

Outsource your end-of-year PAYE filing. Pay your annual PAYE total and N100,000 filing fee — we'll remit the tax and file the return for you within 72 hours.

Monthly VAT Filing (FIRS)

Let us handle your monthly VAT filing with FIRS. Pay the VAT amount and N25,000 service charge — we'll submit on TaxPro Max and return proof of filing within 72 hours.

Monthly WHT Filing (FIRS)

Simplify your monthly WHT obligations. Pay the total WHT due plus N25,000 — we'll handle remittance and filing on TaxPro Max with confirmation delivered within 72 hours.

FIRS Annual Returns Filing

Submit your P&L, pay tax due and a N100,000 filing fee — we'll complete your FIRS annual tax return submission. Filing only, no audit or representation included.

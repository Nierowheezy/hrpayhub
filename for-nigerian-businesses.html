Comprehensive HR Management: Efficient Employee Lifecycle Management

HRPayHub streamlines all HR functions, encompassing employee onboarding, document management, performance reviews, and exit procedures. The platform provides businesses with tools to manage the entire employee lifecycle effectively:

  • Employee Database Management: Maintain a centralized repository for employee records, including personal details, job history, training records, and performance data. This comprehensive database ensures data accuracy, accessibility, and security.
  • Onboarding and Offboarding: Create structured workflows for onboarding new hires and offboarding departing employees. Automated checklists guide HR teams through necessary steps such as document collection, equipment allocation, and exit interviews, ensuring a seamless transition for every employee.
  • 360° Performance Reviews: Implement well-rounded appraisals that gather feedback from multiple sources—peers, supervisors, and subordinates. HRPayHub allows for customized review templates, goal tracking, and automated reminders to facilitate timely assessments

HRPayHub takes the complexity out of payroll processing, ensuring accurate calculations and timely payments across every pay cycle. Key payroll features include:

  • Payslip Generation: Generate and distribute digital payslips with comprehensive salary breakdowns, including deductions, bonuses, overtime, and tax withholdings. Employees can access payslips through the self-service portal, reducing manual inquiries.
  • Payroll Reporting: Produce monthly, quarterly, and annual payroll reports, offering detailed breakdowns of payroll expenses, tax liabilities, and salary disbursements. Reports can be customized to meet regulatory or internal requirements.
  • Bank Upload File Generation: Generate bank upload files for quick and accurate salary disbursements. HRPayHub integrates with leading Nigerian banks, ensuring seamless payroll execution.
  • Payroll Compliance: Stay aligned with regulatory bodies, including the Federal Inland Revenue Service (FIRS) and state tax authorities. HRPayHub automates statutory deductions and generates the necessary reports for tax remittance.
